# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We want InferenceMeter to be a respectful, useful, and technically rigorous community. Be considerate, assume good intent while evaluating evidence, give actionable feedback, and make room for people with different backgrounds and experience levels.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized attention, sustained disruption, publishing another person's private information, or using project spaces for spam or unrelated promotion.

## Scope

This code applies in repository issues, pull requests, reviews, discussions, and other spaces where someone is representing the project or Monomyth Development.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and may warn, temporarily restrict, or permanently ban participants whose behavior is inconsistent with this code. Enforcement decisions will consider context, severity, repetition, and the safety of affected people.

Report conduct concerns privately to `chris@monomyth.dev` with the subject `InferenceMeter conduct report`. Reports will be handled as confidentially as reasonably possible. Do not use public issues for sensitive reports.
